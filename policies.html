What is GDPR?

GDPR is a detailed privacy and data protection law applicable across the European Union (EU). It governs how companies safeguard EU residents’ data and empowers individuals to exercise greater control over their personal information. Importantly, GDPR is not limited to EU-based businesses; it applies to globally operating companies.
